When i first came to University, I had no idea how to code and thought to be impossible for me to learn how to code. It was a big struggle to understand how to code and how I can implement what I have learnt in the lessons into my actual coding for assignments and exams. It was because of the good lectures that teach at the University of Northampton that guided me and helped me learn and understand how to implement the code i learnt into my assignments and exams. The two lecturers that stood out the most has been Mark Johnson (Software Engineering CSY1019) and Chris Rafferty (Web Development CSY1018). They taught me to be patient, calm, and persistent because they knew that this course in particular can be frustrating with code not working or things not planning out the right way. Chris helped me especially with this assignment, gearing me with the knowledge and the mindset I have to have while taking on coding.
